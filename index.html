Web Software Engineer · Morrisville, NC

I studied biochemistry and engineering management in college and worked as an operations manager for 5 years before teaching myself to code. My goal is to be able to deliver complete web applications on Linux systems, so my interests lie in everything from devops automation to application code and producing a functional UI, although I’m definitely not a designer.

My biggest motivator is to always be becoming something new, better, or at the very least, different, in the hope of always challenging myself to see things in new ways. I'm at my best when I know what needs to be done and I know that the only thing in between me and a big goal is a lot of work, but I learn the most about myself when I have no idea what needs to be done and I know that only thing in between me and figuring that out is a period of focused quiet. Balancing those periods of intense action and equally intense quiet in a business environment is my greatest (business) strength, and building that discipline within a team is my value.
